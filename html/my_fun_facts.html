Favorite Book

Ghost In The Wires: My Adventures as the World's Most Wanted Hacker

Ghost in the Wires by Kevin Mitnick is a firsthand account of his life as a notorious hacker. I first got to know about Mitnick after using an application he backed called 'KnowBe4'. The reason he backed this application was to help protect individuals from phishing attacks. In his book, he went over the details of his exploits, social engineering tactics, the hacking culture, etc.
